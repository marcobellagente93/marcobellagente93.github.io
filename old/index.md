# Marco Bellagente
## PhD student at University of Heidelberg and MPIK · marco.bellagente@gmail.com

I am currently in my third-year of PhD in Physics and Deep Learning.
I have many interests, including:
* Bayesian methods for Deep Learning
* Generative Adversarial Networks for high quality event-generation
* Normalizing Flows

I received a Masters degree in Physics (cum laude) from the University of Milan under the supervision of Stefano Forte.
